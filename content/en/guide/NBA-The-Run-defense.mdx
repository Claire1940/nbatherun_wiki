| Ability Name | Effect                               | Activation Trigger                      | Best Use Case                                        |

<Section icon={Swords} title="Team Defense and Coordination in 3v3">
NBA The Run is a 3v3 game, making team defense paramount. Effective communication and coordination with your teammates are essential for locking down opponents and creating turnovers.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Communicate Switches and Screens">
    Actively communicate with your teammates when switching defenders or navigating screens. Miscommunications can lead to open shots or easy drives.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Help Defense and Rotations">
    Be ready to provide help defense when a teammate is beaten off the dribble or is facing a strong post-up player. Quick rotations are key to covering open players.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Pressure the Ball Handler">
    Consistent pressure on the ball handler can disrupt their rhythm, force bad passes, and create opportunities for steals. Work with a teammate to trap if the situation allows.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Control the Paint and Perimeter">
    Assign roles for who primarily defends the paint and who guards the perimeter. This ensures no area is left vulnerable and helps prevent easy scores.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Rebounding Awareness">
    After a shot attempt, immediately switch to rebounding mode. Boxing out opponents and securing defensive rebounds is crucial to ending possessions and preventing second-chance points.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
In Knockout Squads, where you control one player, effective team defense relies heavily on understanding your role and trusting your teammates. In Knockout Solos, you have full control over all three players, allowing for more intricate defensive schemes.
</Callout>

</Section>
