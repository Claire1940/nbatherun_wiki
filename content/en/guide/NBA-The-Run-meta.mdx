<Section icon={Shield} title="Key Mechanics Shaping the NBA The Run Meta">
The core mechanics of NBA The Run are designed to promote a high-skill, arcade-style basketball experience. Understanding these mechanics is vital for anyone looking to dominate the **NBA The Run meta**.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Rollback Netcode">
    NBA The Run utilizes **true rollback netcode** to ensure a smooth and responsive online experience. This minimizes lag and input delay, making competitive online play fair and enjoyable. Responsive controls are essential for executing complex moves like crossovers and alley-oops.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="In the Zone Abilities">
    The **"In the Zone" system** is a momentum-based mechanic that unlocks powerful signature abilities. Performing highlight plays charges your Zone meter faster. Strategically deploying these abilities, such as Posterizer for offense or The Shadow for defense, can turn the tide of a match.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Randomized Rulesets">
    During Knockout Tournaments, players will encounter **randomized rulesets**. These can alter scoring, defensive effectiveness, or even player attributes for a round. Mastering the meta means quickly identifying and exploiting these rule changes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Progression and Cred">
    **Cred** is the in-game currency earned by winning matches. It's used to unlock cosmetics like jerseys, advanced dunks, taunts, badges, banners, and **Rookie Variants**. This progression system allows players to customize their style and show off their achievements.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The interplay between responsive controls, momentum-based abilities, and dynamic rulesets forms the bedrock of the game's competitive depth. Players who can consistently leverage these elements will define the top-tier **NBA The Run meta**.

<Callout type="success" title="Leverage Rollback Netcode">
The implementation of rollback netcode means that every input is critical. Focus on precise timing for shots, blocks, and steals, as the game will accurately reflect your actions without significant delay.
</Callout>

</Section>
