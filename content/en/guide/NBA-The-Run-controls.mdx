<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="On-Ball Defense">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Blocking Shots">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Steals and Interceptions">
    Anticipate passing lanes and dribble patterns to attempt steals. Quick reflexes are essential for picking pockets and intercepting passes, leading to fast break opportunities.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Loose Ball Dives">
    Dive for loose balls to secure possession after deflections or missed shots. This high-risk, high-reward action can be a game-changer, especially in tight matches.
  </Step>
</Steps>
