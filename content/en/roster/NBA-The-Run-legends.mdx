## How to Build a Winning Legends Squad in The Run World Tournament

A lot of players will rush into **NBA The Run legends** by stacking big scorers. That’s exciting, but it usually creates defensive holes and inconsistent possessions. Use role logic instead.

### Recommended lineup skeleton

| Slot | Primary function | Ideal trait mix | Early priority |
|---|---|---|---|
| Lead creator | Initiate offense | Handle + vision + pressure passing | **Very high** |
| Wing scorer | Convert half-court sets | Shot creation + movement shooting | **High** |
| Defensive stopper | Disrupt top threats | Lateral speed + contest timing | **High** |
| Glue forward | Rebounds + screens + switches | Strength + awareness + stamina | **Medium-high** |
| Utility bench piece | Change pace by matchup | Specialized skill (shooting/defense) | **Medium** |

### Chemistry rules to follow

1. **Balance possession types**  
   Have at least two players who can create in transition and one reliable half-court finisher.

2. **Don’t duplicate weaknesses**  
   If two core pieces are weak on-ball defenders, add a pressure defender immediately.

3. **Set a usage hierarchy**  
   Your best scorer should touch often, but your second option must be comfortable initiating when trapped.

4. **Build your “floor” first**  
   Defense, rebounding, and turnover control make your bad games survivable.

For most players, the best **NBA The Run legends** approach is consistency over flash in the first week. You can add flair once your base system is stable.
